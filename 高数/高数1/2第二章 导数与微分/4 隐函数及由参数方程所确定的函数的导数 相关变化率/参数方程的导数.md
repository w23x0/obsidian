### 参数方程导数[[参数方程]]
$$\begin{cases}x=\varphi(t),\\\\y=\psi(t)&\end{cases}$$由[[导数求导法则#和差积商]]][[导数求导法则#反函数的导数求导法则]]$$\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}x}=\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}t}\cdot\frac{\mathrm{d}t}{\mathrm{d}x}=\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}t}\cdot\frac{1}{\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{d}t}}=\frac{\psi^{\prime}(t)}{\varphi^{\prime}(t)},$$
- 一阶$$\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}x}=\frac{\psi^{\prime}(t)}{\varphi^{\prime}(t)}=\frac{\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}t}}{\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{d}t}}.$$
- 二阶$$\frac{\mathrm{d}^2y}{\mathrm{d}x^2}=\frac{\psi^{\prime\prime}(t)\varphi^{\prime}(t)-\psi^{\prime}(t)\varphi^{\prime\prime}(t)}{\varphi^{\prime3}(t)}.$$
- n阶 由[[莱布尼茨公式#导数的乘积法则（Leibniz 求导法则）#商]]**商的形式**$$\frac{d^n}{dt^n}(u\cdot v)=\sum_{k=0}^n\binom{n}{k}\frac{d^ku}{dt^k}\cdot\frac{d^{n-k}v}{dt^{n-k}},$$
### 摆线导数[[摆线]]
[[三角函数公式]]
$$\begin{aligned}&\frac{d_{2}}{d_{2}}={\frac{\mathrm{d}t}{\mathrm{d}x}}={\frac{a\sin t}{a(1-\cos t)}}={\frac{\sin t}{1-\cos t}}=\cot{\frac{t}{2}}\quad(t\neq2n\pi,n\in\mathbf{Z}).\\&\frac{\mathrm{d}^2y}{\mathrm{d}x^2}=\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}t}\left(\cot\frac{t}{2}\right)\cdot\frac{1}{\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{d}t}}=-\frac{1}{2\sin^{2}\frac{t}{2}}\cdot\frac{1}{a(1-\cos t)}\\&=-\frac{1}{a\left(1-\cos t\right)^{2}}\quad(t\neq2n\pi,n\in\mathbf{Z}).\end{aligned}$$

| 拱高   | $( 2r )$                                                    | 从最低到最高       |
