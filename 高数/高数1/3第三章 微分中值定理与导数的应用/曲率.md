弧微分公式
$$ds=\sqrt{1+y^{\prime{2}}}dx.$$
$$ds=\sqrt{x^{\prime{2}}+y^{\prime{2}}}dt.$$

平均曲率
$$\overline{K} = \left|\frac{\Delta\alpha}{\Delta s}\right|.$$

曲率定义
$$
K = \lim_{\Delta s \to 0} \left|\frac{\Delta\alpha}{\Delta s}\right|.$$
$$K = \left|\frac{d\alpha}{ds}\right|.$$

直角坐标方程
$$K = \frac{|y^{\prime\prime}|}{\left(1+y^{\prime{2}}\right)^{3/2}}.$$

参数方程
$$K=\frac{\mid\varphi^{\prime}(\:t\:)\psi^{\prime\prime}(\:t\:)-\varphi^{\prime\prime}(\:t\:)\psi^{\prime}(\:t\:)\mid}{\left[\:\varphi^{\prime2}(\:t\:)+\psi^{\prime2}(\:t\:)\:\right]^{3/2}}.$$

极坐标方程
$$K = \frac{\mid r^{2}+2r^{\prime{2}}-rr^{\prime\prime} \mid}{\left(r^{2}+r^{\prime{2}}\right)^{3/2}}.$$

曲率半径
$$\rho = \frac{1}{K}.$$
